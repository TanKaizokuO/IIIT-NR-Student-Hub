ExpenseBud Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30.11.2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app, **ExpenseBud**.

Information We Collect

ExpenseBud collects the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, and other contact details when you register.
  • Financial Data: Expense and income details that you input into the app for tracking purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including device type, IP address, and activity logs.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the functionality of the ExpenseBud app.
  • Analyze and improve our app's performance.
  • Customize your experience and provide insights about your expenses.
  • Send important updates or notifications about the app.

Third-Party Services

ExpenseBud may use third-party services for analytics or advertising. These services may collect cookies or similar technologies to provide a better experience. We ensure that these third parties comply with relevant privacy laws.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

ExpenseBud uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie usage through your device settings. Please note that disabling cookies may impact the app's functionality.

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using encryption and other best practices. We take all necessary measures to prevent unauthorized access, but no method of transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

ExpenseBud does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time.
  • Opt out of certain third-party data-sharing practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, with the updated date noted above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
